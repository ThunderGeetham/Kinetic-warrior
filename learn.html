It all started when a 11 year old boy kept on forgeting his pencil to school everyday and had to borrow one from his friends.
Even though he had a pencil, he hated it as he had to switch between pen and pencil every 10 seconds.
So one day he got an idea of making a 2 in 1(pen + pencil) pen with some useless pen caps, broken pencil and a pen and made the WARRIOR.
It was also good in PenFights(WWE of pens).
One day his friend loved the pen so much that he partnered up with him and added an eraser.
After few days, they made two companies called KINETIC and Shinobi.
Kinetic created the pens and Shinobi marketed and sold'em.
They started selling them in school and it was pretty good.
Now they are selling nationaly in India.
As you read this, a new model is being designed and made by KINETIC.
